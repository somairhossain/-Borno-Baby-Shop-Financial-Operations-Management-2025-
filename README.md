The spreadsheet is designed to manage day-to-day business activities, including:

* Expense tracking (daily & monthly)
* Cash flow monitoring
* Advance payments (Cash / bKash)
* Supplier payments
* Product purchase records
* Staff salary management
* Advertising costs (e.g., Facebook Ads)
* Courier & carriage expenses
* Inventory movement (Stock In / Stock Out)
* Invoice and order tracking
* District & Thana database for logistics
* Monthly financial summaries

This system provides operational clarity, financial transparency, and real-time visibility into business performance.
